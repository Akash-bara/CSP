Food Adulteration Awareness at Sri Chaitanya Jr College

Event Summary

We visited Sri Chaitanya Jr College to educate students about the dangers of food adulteration. Our main goal was to create awareness about identifying adulterated food and promoting safe consumption practices.

Demonstration with Salt

We conducted a simple experiment to show how to distinguish between iodized salt and pure salt. This hands-on demonstration helped students understand the importance of using safe food products.

Awareness Activities

Students actively participated in the demonstration and asked questions. We discussed the harmful effects of adulterated food, the types of adulteration, and preventive measures they should follow at home.
